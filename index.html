About Us

Chai Adda Café is a local hangout spot in Rohini, inspired by the classic Indian chai tapri. We use the finest ingredients and traditional recipes to serve comfort snacks and beverages, just like home. Our cozy ambiance and friendly staff make it the perfect place for a quick bite, a long chat, or a peaceful moment over chai.
